Our Story

Ideal Surveys was established in 1990 by the current owner, who has over 30 years experience in the industry. We are a family run business and pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and attention to detail. We have a team of highly skilled and experienced surveyors who are all CSCS qualified and fully insured. We are based in the South East of England and cover the whole of the UK.

Our Services

We offer a range of services including topographical surveys, measured building surveys, and setting out services. We use the latest technology and equipment to ensure that we provide the most accurate and detailed surveys possible. We work with a wide range of clients including architects, engineers, developers, and private individuals. No job is too big or too small for us, and we are committed to providing a high quality service at a competitive price.
